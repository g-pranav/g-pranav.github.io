about me

I'm a robotics enthusiast with a specialization in mobile robots, backed by hands-on experience in both hardware and software development.

I am originally from India where I completed my bachelors in mechanical engineering. I was actually very interested in Automotive engineering initially when I joined bachelors. With time my interests shifted to robotics. I have recently completed my masters in Robotics and autonomous systems from the University of Lübeck with a focus on mobile robotics, navigation and control. During this time I worked at the Institute for Robotics and Cognitive Systems at the University of Lübeck with different roles.
